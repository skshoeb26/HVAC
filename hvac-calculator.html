Design Assumptions
  1. Inside Condition: 23°C ± 2°F, RH ~50 ± 5% (73.4°F DB, 61.2°F WB)
  2. Outside Condition: 42°C (107.6°F DB, 36% RH, 83°F WB, TH=46.75)
  3. Lighting Load: 1.1 Watts / Sq.ft (default, adjustable per room)
  4. Fresh Air: 1 Air Change per hour OR (5.0 cfm/person + 0.06 CFM/Sqft), whichever is higher
  5. Equipment Load: user-entered in kW (150 Watts per PC typical)
  6. Occupancy as per Architectural Layout
  7. Exposed Glass — Single Pane with Venetian Blind or Roller Shade (U=0.33)
  8. External walls U-factor = 0.34 BTU/(hr·ft²·°F); Partition walls U = 0.40
  9. Roof U-factor = 0.12, CLTD = 51.2°F (includes solar gain)
  10. Internal partitions / ceiling / floor CLTD = 29.2°F
  11. Floor below considered Non-AC area
  12. ADP (Apparatus Dew Point) = 54°F — fixed for Indian commercial HVAC
  13. Peak Load Time: April @ 16:00 HRS
  14. ERSH safety factor: 10% | ERLH safety factor: 5% | GTH safety factor: 3%
  15. FA sensible = FA_CFM × DIFF_db × (0.15 × 1.08) | FA latent = FA_CFM × DIFF_grLb × (0.15 × 0.67)
  16. FA outside heat = FA_CFM × DIFF_th × (4.5 × 0.85)
  17. Dehumidifier CFM = ERSH_total / (1.08 × (T_room − ADP)) = ERSH / (1.08 × 19.4)
